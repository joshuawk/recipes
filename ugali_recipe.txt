Ingredients
===========

1. 350 grams of maize flour.
2. A sufuria.
3. Water.
4. Fire.
5. A wooden spoon.
6. A 500 ml cup.

Process
=======

1. Add 1500 ml of water into the sufuria.
2. Bring the water to boil.
3. Lower the heat.
4. Add the maize flour and stir with the wooden spoon until the mixture starts solidifying.
5. Invert the mixture so that it can get cooked all round.
6. Remove the ugali from the cooker.

This serves four people.
